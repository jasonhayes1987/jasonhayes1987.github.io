About PhoenX RL

PhoenX RL is a powerful tool designed for AI researchers and reinforcement learning engineers. It enables rapid prototyping and testing of RL agents in customizable Gymnasium and Isaac Sim environments, accelerating the development of intelligent systems. Built with flexibility in mind, it integrates seamlessly with PyTorch, Ray, and WANDB.
